About The CEO

Hello I'm Olarinde Olayemi Ayanfeoluwa, a student of the department of Microbiology, University of Lagos.

A complete art lover and fashion enthusiast. A big fan of depth in practically anything. I love dancing, travelling, thinking, gisting and designing.

I've been able to conceive luluarts from my soul, in other words, lulu arts entails me in general.



About Lulu Arts Inc.

Luluarts was established on the 30th of December, 2015 with the aim to capture souls, create a network between artists and art lovers or beginners, basically to channel/impact knowledge into them. Also its been set aside as a work space and gallery for art showcase (visual art, photography etc).

Luluarts consists of a few affiliates like; Un.earthical (photography), Charity with art(humanity) and Shako X Lulu (fashion).

In 2015, I ( luluarts) have bagged two awards (Unilag Nigma awards as the most creative student) and (University of Lagos science student awards as the most enterprising female) and have been nominated for 3

We are luluarts, and we offer a list of services which includes;

  • Art commissions and contracts
  • Fashion
  • Photography
  • Poetry
  • Graphics/digital art
  • Branding/Promotion
  • And outdoor art jobs like (speed painting, face painting etc).
